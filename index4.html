About Us

Sweet & Spicy events ensures that it provides a fast, efficient, simple and enjoyable online experience to customers. Customers can select from a wide range of party essentials and packages. From tables, chairs, glassware, cutlery, crockery and many other functional items that anyone needs to host a successful party, to functional extras like portable heater, portable fans, lighting, and tents. Services offered are: Complete package which has all party essentials, tent, portable heater and portable fan. Family package includes all party essentials but tables, tents, portable heather nor portable fan. Couples package is simple with one table, 2 chairs and the respective party essentials. Finally our Special Package includes half of the amount of items provided for the complete package.
